Tic Tac Toe Game

Enter "X" or "O" in the boxes. Determine the winner yourself!


How to Play:

  • Two players take turns manually entering "X" and "O".
  • After each move, players visually check if someone has three in a row.
  • Use the "Clear Board" button to start a new game.

Winner:

First to complete a row, column, or diagonal wins!
